About

I am an MS by Research student at the Robotics Research Centre, IIIT Hyderabad, working under Dr. Harikumar Kandath and Dr. Tejas Bodas.

My research focuses on the theoretical underpinnings of reinforcement learning and non-convex optimization. I am particularly drawn to the mathematics that governs how learning systems converge — and the fascinating cases where they don't.

I have a deep appreciation for mathematical analysis and probability theory as tools for understanding complex learning phenomena.

Outside of research, I love watching and supporting Manchester United and following Alexander Volkanovski's journey in MMA. I also enjoy cooking new dishes, travelling to new places, and long conversations with friends.
